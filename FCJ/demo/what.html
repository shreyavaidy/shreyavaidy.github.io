What? and How?

Nearly 80% of U.S. equities are traded using algorithms today.
The greatest portion of the algo-trading is high frequency trading (HFT), which attempts to capitalize on placing a large number of orders at very fast speeds across multiple markets and multiple decision parameters, based on pre-programmed instructions. This pre-programmed code is able to execute orders within micro-seconds, something that a regular trader could have only dreamed of. So most of the humans in the trading floor are already taken over by these algorithms that are operated by someone.

Efficiency Gains and High Speed is the name of the game
